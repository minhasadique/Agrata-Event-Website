ABOUT US

Agrata stands as a beacon of excellence, dedicated to shaping the future leaders of the management and business world.

Agrata is more than just an event, It's a platform designed to empower the students by enhancing their management acumen and fostering a diverse skill set beyond traditional paradigms. With a strong focus on personal and professional growth. Agrata promises to deliver a day brimming with enlightening experiences and boundless opportunities.

Event Highlights:

Stalls and Activities:

Agrata 2024 will feature many stalls offering a diverse range of experiences including food, games, art & craft, merchandise and information & awareness stalls

Networking and engagement :

This event offers a unique opportunity to connect with the future leaders of the industry, fostering collaboration and idea exchange among a diverse group of participants.
